ABOUT US...

Synergy tech d.o.o. was founded in 2015, as a private company, capable of providing efficient and quality services in the aforementioned areas. The team of workers consisting of engineers, certified welders and qualified mechanical workers represent one of the biggest advantages of the company.

Over the years, the company has systematically developed its way of working, investing in education and human resources, new tools and machinery. Special emphasis is placed on the education of welders, acceptance and implementation of the latest technologies.

Since its foundation, pipeline construction has been developed and treated as the core business of the company. Synergy tech d.o.o. specializes in a whole range of activities related to the construction of pipelines of transport and industrial plants, of different diameters and for different media.

Pipeline construction has been brought to an extremely high level in terms of work organization, quality of execution and compliance with deadlines. The company is able to immediately implement all the necessary welding technologies in the construction of pipelines, whether carbon steel, stainless steel or polyethylene.

All the company's work is performed in accordance with ISO standards, for which it has valid certificates.
